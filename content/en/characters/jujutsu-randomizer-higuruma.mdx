The strongest habits are easy to remember:

- **Use control before burst.**
- **Use movement before panic.**
- **Use domains when the fight is crowded.**
- **Use healing after you break line of pressure, not in the open.**

Two passives are especially useful to understand because they change how a roll feels in real time. **Big Brother** improves blood-based skills and removes their HP cost, while **Vessel** improves Black Flash damage. If you see those passives, treat them as signals that your kit has a stronger synergy path than usual.

A beginner should also respect self-cost skills. **Black Hole** is a last-resort play that only works at low HP, and blood-style attacks can tax your health if you press them too carelessly. Use them when the trade is worth it, not just because the animation looks cool.
