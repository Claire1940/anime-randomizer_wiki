<Section icon={Target} title="Boss Fight Strategy & Walkthrough">

Defeating Haruta requires a systematic approach. Because his Miracles technique can bypass fatal damage, players who rely solely on slow, high-damage ultimate abilities will find themselves frustrated as Haruta repeatedly survives with a sliver of health. 

Follow this step-by-step combat strategy to neutralize his luck and secure a quick victory.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation & Build Setup">
    Equip weapons or Cursed Techniques that feature high hit-rates or damage-over-time (DoT) effects, such as Blood Manipulation or Disaster Flames. Avoid heavy, single-hit burst builds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Phase 1: Neutralizing the Hand Sword">
    Haruta will begin the fight by throwing his Hand Sword to distract you. Do not target the sword itself; keep your focus on Haruta while using mobility skills to dodge the crawling blade's grasp.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Phase 3: Depleting the Miracle Marks">
    Engage Haruta with rapid multi-hit combos. Each time you land what would be a fatal blow, one of his lilac facial marks will turn white, indicating a Miracle has been consumed. You must deplete all six marks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Phase: Cornering the Coward">
    Once his Miracle marks are fully depleted, Haruta's defense drops significantly, and he will attempt to flee. Use crowd control (CC) skills or domain expansions to trap him and finish the fight.
  </Step>
</Steps>

During the fight, keep a close eye on the visual indicators under his eyes. If the marks are glowing white, his survival passive is active, and attempting to burst him down will only waste your valuable resources.

<Callout type="warning" title="Fatal Blow Bypass">
Never use your highest-cooldown ultimate skill if Haruta has more than three active purple marks. Save your ultimate for the final phase when his marks are entirely white.
</Callout>
