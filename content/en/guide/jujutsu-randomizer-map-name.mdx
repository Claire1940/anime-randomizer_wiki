<Section icon={Target} title="How to Read the Arena Fast">

If you want stronger results, treat every round like a quick scan exercise. The goal is to identify where you can move, where enemies can corner you, and where your best skills will actually land.

<Callout type="tip" title="Fast-Read Rule">
Open lanes are usually safer for mobility skills, while tight spaces are better for stuns, knockback, and domain pressure.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check your spawn position">
    Look for open space, nearby walls, and the closest enemy angle. Your first move should make sense for the kit you rolled.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Classify your skills">
    Decide whether you have burst, mobility, control, defense, or a risky finisher. That decision changes how you approach the map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Pick your movement lane">
    Use open paths if you have dashes or teleport tools. Use corners only if your kit rewards close-range pressure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Take the first safe trade">
    Do not rush the nearest target unless your opener is strong. A clean first hit is better than a messy brawl.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Arena focus | What to watch | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Spawn edge | Nearby enemies and exit space | Prevents early collapse |
| Open lane | Dash paths and projectile angles | Best for repositioning |
| Tight corner | Stuns, knockback, and domains | High damage, high risk |
| Reset zone | Space to heal or wait cooldowns | Helps you survive longer |

A clean arena read gives you free value. Even a strong roll can lose if you sprint into a narrow trap with no escape route.
