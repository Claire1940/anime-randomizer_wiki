<Section icon={Target} title="Core Combo Paths and Counters">

The easiest way to win more rounds is to build every combo around one question: what keeps the enemy in place long enough for the finisher to land? That is why stun, swap, and domain skills matter so much in this game.

<Callout type="warning" title="Spacing decides trades">
If you push directly into a ready burst move, you often lose the exchange before your own attack even starts.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open with control">
    Start with **Frame Punch**, **Fast Reaction baiting**, or a position tool like **Boogie Woogie**. Your first job is to create a stable opening.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Lock the target">
    Follow the opener with a melee chain or a short gap closer such as **Lucky Uppercut**, **Instant Zap**, or **Surprise Attack**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spend the burst">
    Use your strongest hit next: **Hollow Purple**, **Furnace**, **Angry Barrage**, or a domain when multiple enemies are nearby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset or escape">
    If the hit does not secure the round, fall back with **Aqua Burst**, **24 FPS Dash**, or **Simple Domain** before you get punished.
  </Step>
</Steps>
